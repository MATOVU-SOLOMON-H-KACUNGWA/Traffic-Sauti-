KEEP IN MIND! AS YOU USE THE ROAD

General Traffic Safety Tips

  • Always wear your seatbelt when driving or riding in a vehicle.
  • Obey traffic laws and speed limits.
  • Avoid using your phone while driving.
  • Keep a safe following distance from the vehicle in front of you.
  • Use turn signals to indicate your intentions to other drivers.

Pedestrian Safety Tips

  • Always use crosswalks when crossing the street.
  • Look both ways before crossing the road, even at marked crosswalks.
  • Make eye contact with drivers before stepping into the street.
  • Stay visible at night by wearing reflective clothing.

Bicycle Safety Tips

  • Wear a helmet to protect your head in case of a fall.
  • Obey traffic laws when riding on the road.
  • Use hand signals to indicate turns and stops.
  • Equip your bike with lights and reflectors for visibility.
